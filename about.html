Our Story

On 30th March, a dream turned into reality when two best friends, Amit Koushal and Sanjay Koushal, came together to build more than just a coaching center — they built a temple of learning called Vasu Coaching Classes.

With a shared passion for education and a strong belief in the power of guidance, they laid the foundation of this institute not just with bricks and books, but with hope, trust, and heart. Their friendship is the backbone of our institute — and their dedication is the soul.

Our Mission

We believe that every student is more than just a roll number — they are family. We aim to provide not only academic guidance but also emotional support, motivation, and mentorship. This is not just a coaching center — This is where futures are shaped. This is where dreams take flight.
